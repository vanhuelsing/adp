Effective: March 10, 2026

apideals.ai is an AI API price comparison platform operated by Orqestral UG (haftungsbeschränkt). We display publicly available pricing data for large language models. This policy explains what data we handle and why.

Short version: we collect almost nothing. No cookies, no tracking, no analytics, no user accounts.

1. What We Collect

We do not actively collect personal data through the website. Here's what may be processed:

  • Server logs — Our hosting provider (Vercel) automatically logs IP addresses, request timestamps, and browser user-agent strings. These logs are generated for security and operational purposes and are not accessed or analyzed by us under normal circumstances.
  • Email address — If you click "Get in touch," your email client opens a mailto: link. We only receive your email address if you choose to send us a message. We do not use contact forms or collect emails otherwise.

We do not use cookies, tracking pixels, analytics tools, or fingerprinting. There are no user accounts or login functionality.

2. How We Use Data

  • Server logs — Infrastructure security and abuse prevention (legitimate interest, Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR).
  • Email communication — To respond to your inquiry (Art. 6(1)(b) GDPR — pre-contractual measures, or Art. 6(1)(f) — legitimate interest in responding to contact requests).

We do not sell, rent, or share personal data for advertising or marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following infrastructure providers who may process data on our behalf:

  • Vercel Inc. (San Francisco, USA) — Website hosting and edge delivery. Vercel processes server logs including IP addresses. Data transfers to the US are covered by Vercel's Data Processing Agreement and EU Standard Contractual Clauses. Vercel Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase Inc. (San Francisco, USA) — Database for storing publicly available LLM pricing data. No personal user data is stored in Supabase. Supabase Privacy Policy.

We do not embed third-party scripts, social media widgets, or advertising networks.

  • Data portability — Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format (Art. 20).
  • Objection — Object to processing based on legitimate interest (Art. 21).
